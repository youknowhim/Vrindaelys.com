The Eternity Story

A table beside falling water.

Tucked into Town Hall Square, Eternity began with a simple idea — that a café could feel like an escape.

The waterfall at its heart turns the everyday into something cinematic. Guests come for the avocado toast and stay for the stillness; they book high tea for a birthday and leave planning the next. For more than a decade, it has been Sydney's quiet ritual — a place the city returns to.

— Eternity Café, est. Sydney
